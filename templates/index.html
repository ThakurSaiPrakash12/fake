Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Deepfake?

A deepfake is an AI-generated video or audio recording that realistically replaces someone's likeness or voice with another person's.

How Does Detection Work?

Our AI analyzes microscopic visual and audio inconsistencies that human eyes can't detect, identifying manipulated content.

Is My Data Safe?
